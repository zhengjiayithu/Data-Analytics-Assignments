The City & The Data

We used data provided by Yelp to perform an in-depth analysis of restaurants, reviews, and yelp users in Montreal to give us an inclusive views. The data include many business attributes, e.g., stars, parking availability, categories. It also includes reviews for all restaurants and information about users who wrote these reviews. We used the data and provided search function below for you to try. In addition, we used machine learning techniques to provide further recommendations to users based on their characteristics.
